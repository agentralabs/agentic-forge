# Experience With vs Without AgenticForge

## Without AgenticForge

- Architecture decisions are delayed and fragmented.
- Dependency and wiring inconsistencies surface late.
- Rework increases as implementation diverges from intent.

## With AgenticForge

- Blueprint-first architecture before coding.
- Consistent entity/dependency/wiring model.
- Faster implementation with lower structural rework risk.
